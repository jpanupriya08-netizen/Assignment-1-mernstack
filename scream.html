The Scream

Artist: Edvard Munch

Year of Creation: 1893

Category: Expressionism

The Scream is one of the most recognizable artworks in modern art history. Created by Edvard Munch, the painting represents human anxiety, fear, and emotional struggle. The dramatic sky and distorted figure create a powerful expression of inner feelings.
