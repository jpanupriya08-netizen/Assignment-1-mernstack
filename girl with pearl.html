Girl With a Pearl Earring

Artist: Johannes Vermeer

Year of Creation: 1665

Category: Baroque Art

Girl With a Pearl Earring is one of Johannes Vermeer's most famous paintings. Often called the "Mona Lisa of the North", this portrait is admired for its beautiful use of light, color, and the mysterious expression of the young woman wearing a large pearl earring.
