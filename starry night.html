The Starry Night

Artist: Vincent van Gogh

Year of Creation: 1889

Category: Post-Impressionism

The Starry Night is one of Vincent van Gogh's most famous paintings. Created during his stay at an asylum in France, the artwork shows a peaceful village beneath a swirling night sky filled with stars. It is known for its expressive brush strokes and emotional depth.
