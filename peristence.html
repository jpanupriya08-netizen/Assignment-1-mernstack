The Persistence of Memory

Artist: Salvador Dalí

Year of Creation: 1931

Category: Surrealism

The Persistence of Memory is one of Salvador Dalí's most famous surrealist paintings. The artwork shows melting clocks in a strange dream-like landscape, exploring ideas about time, memory, and reality. It is known for its imaginative style and symbolic meaning.
